Instructions for Camera-ready Submission

The camera-ready copies must be received by Dec. 19th EST, 2012.

Format

Your submission is limited to ten (10) 8.5×11 single spaced, double-column pages, using 10pt font. Each paper may have up to two extra pages; each extra page will be charged for US$100. The page limit includes everything: references, title, figures, appendices, abstract, etc.

Your submission must be formatted for black-and-white printers and not color printers. This is especially true for plots and graphs in the paper.

Do NOT number the pages.

If you use Latex, you must use this template .  The template is the same as the template required in our call-for-paper, but with some minor modifications to the copyright area. Note, the latex template defaults to 9pt; please change it to 10pt.

Be sure to remove the \documentclass[preprint]{sigplanconf} and replace with \documentclass[10pt]{cgo13}.

An example of the usage of the template is here.

If you use word, you must use this template .

Your final submission must be a PDF file.

Categories and Terms

Please use the proper categories shown here and terms shown here .

Copyright Clearance

Please send your signed copyright form by Dec. 19 EST 2012 to Xipeng Shen (xshen@cs.wm.edu). Please put [CGO'13] and your paper ID# in the subject. The copyright form can be found here .

The appropriate copyright clearance code notice must be put on the bottom of the first page of the paper:

* For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government,
the copyright notice is: U.S. Government work not protected by
U.S. copyright

* For papers in which all authors are employed by a Crown government
(UK, Canada, and Australia), the copyright notice is:
978-1-4673-5525-4/13/$31.00 ©2013 Crown

* For all other papers the copyright notice is:
978-1-4673-5525-4/13/$31.00 ©2013 IEEE

For example, if you use the Latex template, you need to add the
following right below \begin{document}:

\conferenceinfo{CGO’13}{23-27 February 2013, Shenzhen China.}
\copyrightyear{2013}
\copyrightdata{978-1-4673-5525-4/13/\$31.00 \copyright2013 IEEE}

If you use the word template, you must ensure that the following appear in the bottom copyright area:

Permission to make digital or hard copies of all or part of this work for personal or classroom use is granted without fee provided that copies are not made or distributed for profit or commercial advantage and that copies bear this notice and the full citation on the first page. To copy otherwise, or republish, to post on servers or to redistribute to lists, requires prior specific permission and/or a fee.
CGO’13    23-27 February 2013, Shenzhen China.
978-1-4673-5525-4/13/$31. ©2013IEEE…$15.00

File Naming

Name the pdf file using your submission ID# with the lead (first) author’s last name Others (example: 101_Zhou.pdf.)

Submission

The paper must be submitted in PDF. It must pass the IEEE PDF Express Format Check at here . Please find how to use the online format checking system in this document .  After passing the online format check, you can submit the final version by just clicking the “Approve for Collection” link in that format checking webpage.

Others

Addition/removal of authors once the paper is accepted will have to be approved by the program chairs, Kathryn S McKinley(mckinley@cs.utexas.edu) and Lieven Eeckhout(leeckhou@elis.ugent.be).

Please contact Xipeng Shen (xshen@cs.wm.edu) for any questions regarding the final version submission.
